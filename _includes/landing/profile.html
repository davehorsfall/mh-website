Why work with me?

My name is Mel, and I am a qualified practitioner with post-graduate level training and professional registration. My therapeutic practice developed after working for over twenty years in education, with over ten years as a specialist SEND teacher. I work holistically with the child at the centre, in a way that is both permissive and develops a sense of responsibility. I have a deep understanding of child development and am both attachment & trauma-informed. I have a specific interest in neurodiversity and have worked with Autistic children and their families for over ten years. Play Therapy offers expression through play and unconditional acceptance of all brain functions and is therefore inherently neuro-affirming.

  • Child‑centred creative arts and play therapy for ages 4–13.
  • School‑based programmes and group interventions to support emotional wellbeing.
  • Parent and carer consultations with practical guidance for home support.
  • Holistic, trauma‑informed and neurodiversity‑affirming approach.
  • Collaborative work with families, teachers and professionals.
