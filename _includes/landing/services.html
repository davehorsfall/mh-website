Play Therapy Services

Helping children aged 4–13 express themselves, build resilience, and thrive emotionally, with support for parents, carers, and schools.

Private Sessions

One-to-one play therapy for children 4–13, using creative arts to process emotions, build resilience, and support neurodiverse needs, with parent consultation to extend therapy at home.

Schools Service

Play therapy within schools, offering 1:1 or group support to help children manage emotions, build social skills, and thrive academically, tailored to developmental and neurodiverse needs.

Parent Support

Guidance for parents and carers to understand and support children’s emotional wellbeing, providing strategies, insight, and practical tools to strengthen relationships and manage challenging behaviours effectively.
